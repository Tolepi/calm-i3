# calm-i3
This is my calm i3 config, hope you actually like it.

Welcome and thank you so much for reading, this i3 rice specifically is special for me, since im a big lover of linux ricing, styling and linux in general, no vibecoded readme, (tho parts of the rice are vibecoded sadly :/) so let me tell you what has happened to me and what inspired me to make this linux rice.

Recently, ive been dealing with anxiety problems, my life has been quite a bit horrible, ive been taking medication and it doesnt seem to work no matter how many time has passed, and my anxiety has worsened every day. I basically get anxious and some other horrible feelings for basically nothing now. Now everything can make me anxious and i cant seemly get better. I have approximately 7 suicide attempts, none could work/my parents found me when i was about to do it, i also got bullied in middle school (which i am still in, i havent got to high school yet) that just made things worse. But one day, in a random evening, i found linux, ricing, and technology in general, it became my refugee. I consider this rice a big part of me, makes me calm, makes me feel... Right. Makes me feel like its my masterpiece, even though its not the biggest repo ever, even though the repo is kind of small, even though i havent really invented these types of rices, it just feels like its my safe, quiet and calm space. For you, it might be a simple rice and its just that. But for me, its my personal space and the thing that makes all my hopes go up a lot. May add more stuff for this repo if needed. I love yall, remember to be safe out there and keep ricing. Thank you! -Tolepi
